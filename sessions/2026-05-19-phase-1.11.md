## Decisions made

- **Hybrid typographic register** · *Clover confirmed* — Smokum (American personality, corner masthead), Abril Fatface (Victorian authority, poem title), IM Fell English (17-18c English printing matrices, poem body), Playfair Display SC (attribution/labels). British-register-only alternative (Abril Fatface masthead) and American-register-only alternative (Smokum masthead + Libre Bodoni body) both prototyped and rejected in favour of the hybrid.

- **Fonts loaded site-wide as shared toolbox** · *design call* — new fonts are in `base.html` unconditionally, not scoped to a print-only stylesheet. Rationale: these fonts will be used in the web typography pass (pre-beta style session); loading them once avoids a second Google Fonts request later.

- **Turnover (runover) indent** · *design call* — `padding-left: 2em; text-indent: -2em` on `.poem-line-out`. First physical line flush left; wrapped continuations indented 2em. Standard poetry-print convention. `overflow-wrap: break-word` as last-resort fallback for pathologically long names.

- **Horse name separation via underline gap** · *design call* — `display: inline; white-space: nowrap` on `.poem-horse` (not `display: inline-block`, which creates a new formatting context and allows text to wrap internally). The gap in the underline at each inter-name space is the visual separator. Thin warm-gray (`#c0ad90`, `0.5pt`) to be present but not dominant.

- **Corner logo layout** · *Clover call* — after printing the first version, Clover found everything too small and the stacked masthead/title wasted vertical space. Switched to 2-column grid: Smokum corner logo left, title centered in remaining column. Size scales: body/headers ×150%, colophon/permalink ×200%.

- **Fence ornament deferred** · *Clover + Claude agreed* — picket fence as a print ornamental rule/separator discussed and held. Aesthetic mismatch with the plain-text typographic register; also the fence belongs to the illustrative register, not the typographic ornament register (fleurons, rules). Reserved for Phase 2 broadsheet mode where decorative weight is matched throughout.

- **Colophon wording** · *Clover proposed* — "Composed entirely from the names of real, registered horses." with permalink below. Explains the poem's constraint to readers encountering a printed copy without site context.
