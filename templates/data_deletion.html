Data Deletion

Last updated: 2026-05-15

How to delete your data

You have two scopes of deletion available.

Delete your account

Email the operator (see address below) from the email address associated with your sign-in and ask for account deletion. We will:

  • Remove your user record (slug, display name, profile links, preferences).
  • Remove your private collections (stable, pasture, saved poems, saved horses).
  • Remove your drafts.
  • Either delete your published poems entirely, or — if you prefer — keep them on the site as anonymous with attribution stripped. State your preference in the email.
  • Revoke your Clerk session.

Account deletion is processed within seven days. We will reply when it is complete.

Delete an individual poem

Use the Report button on the poem's permalink page and select "I am the author and want this removed". You can also email the operator with the poem URL.

Anonymous submissions

Anonymous poems carry no link to any account, so we generally cannot verify authorship. Email the operator with the poem URL and as much context as you can; we will use our best judgement.

Local data only

If you have never signed in, all of your poet.horse data lives in your own browser's local storage. Clearing site data for poet.horse in your browser settings removes it completely; we hold nothing.

Contact

Email the operator via the contact link in the footer. Include the email address associated with your Clerk sign-in.

This page satisfies the data-deletion instructions requirement for OAuth providers that ask for one. A signed-request webhook will be added when a connected provider requires it.
