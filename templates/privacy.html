Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-15

The short version

  • We don't run analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party fingerprinting on poet.horse.
  • If you sign in, we store: your Clerk user ID, the slug and display name you chose, the poems you publish, the horses in your stable and pasture, and your site preferences.
  • If you don't sign in, we store nothing about you on our servers. Drafts and your stable live in your browser's local storage.
  • Your email lives with Clerk (our authentication provider). We never see your password.
  • Standard web server logs (IP, user agent, request path) are kept short-term for spam and abuse handling.
  • You can delete your account and your data at any time. See Data Deletion.

The longer version

1. What we collect

If you sign in we store, in our own database:

  • A Clerk-issued user identifier.
  • The slug, display name, profile links, and role you chose.
  • Poems you submit, with the title, lines, tags, and any attribution you supplied.
  • Horses you add to your stable, pasture, or saved collections.
  • Preferences (e.g. page size, reader-mode, signing name) you've set.

If you don't sign in we store nothing on our servers about you personally. Your in-progress poems, your stable, and your preferences are kept in your browser's local storage and never sent to us until you choose to submit a poem.

2. Authentication

Sign-in is handled by Clerk (clerk.com/privacy). We receive a verified user identifier and a display name from your identity provider. We do not see your password.

3. Server logs

Our web server keeps short-term access logs containing the request path, the response status, your IP address, and your user-agent string. These logs are used for debugging, rate limiting, and abuse handling. They are not shared with third parties and are rotated.

4. Anonymous submissions

You can submit poems anonymously. Anonymous poems are permanently anonymous; there is no path to retroactively claim them.

5. Public information

Your published poems, display name, slug, and profile links are public.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie for keeping you signed in. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

7. Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. Cross-posting to platforms like Tumblr only happens when an admin flags a poem for cross-post, and only the poem content and attribution you supplied are sent.

8. Deletion

You can delete your account and your poems at any time. See Data Deletion.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced on the home page.
