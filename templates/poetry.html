How it works

  1. Search for horses. Type a word or pattern in the search box — *dance* matches anything containing "dance", dance* starts with it, * dance ends with it. Or use Short names to browse 3-letter-or-fewer horses, Random to get a surprise batch, or My pasture to pull from your saved collection.
  2. Add to stable. Click + stable next to any horse to move it to your working stable on the right.
  3. Build the poem. Drag horses from the stable into poem lines, or click a horse chip to open its menu. Each horse can appear as many times as it shows up in the dictionary.
  4. Post. When you're happy, click Post poem to give your poem a title and submit it for review.

Wildcard syntax: dance exact   dance* starts   *dance ends   *dance* anywhere   dance * first word   * dance last word
